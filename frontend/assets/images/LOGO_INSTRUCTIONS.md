# Logo Setup Instructions

Please save your PThrive logo image as:
`frontend/assets/images/pthrive_logo.png`

The logo should be:
- PNG format (transparent background preferred)
- Recommended size: 512x512 pixels or larger
- The logo will be automatically resized in the app

After saving the logo file, the app will display it on:
- Splash Screen
- Login Screen
- Register Screen
